Legal Secondary Sources

Getting Started

Legal Encyclopedias

Broad overviews of legal topics arranged alphabetically, like Am Jur or CJS.

Treatises

In-depth, scholarly books focusing exclusively on a single area of law.

Legal Dictionaries

Definitions of legal terms and phrases.

Specialized Secondary Sources

Law Reviews and Law Journals

Scholarly articles analyzing niche legal issues, published by law schools.

ALR Annotations

Articles that organize and analyze cases on specific legal points across jurisdictions.

Restatements

Highly authoritative summaries of common law principles compiled by experts.

Form Books

Collections of sample legal documents, pleadings, and transactional templates.

Practice Guides

Jurisdiction-specific roadmaps explaining how to litigate or handle legal tasks.

CLE Materials

Handouts and outlines from Continuing Legal Education courses for practicing attorneys.

Looseleaf Services

Frequently updated binders tracking volatile fields like tax or environmental law.
